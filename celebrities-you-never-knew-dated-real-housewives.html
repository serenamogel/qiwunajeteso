Celebrities You Never Knew Dated Real Housewives

September 26, 2024
blog

Don't be fooled by the name of the show — Cary Deuber of The Real Housewives of Dallas works outside of the home and has a successful career as a nurse in the plastic surgery practice of her husband, Dr. Mark Deuber. The pair appear to be mostly happily married, a rock of stability in the often chaotic universe of the Texas-set version of the Real Housewives franchise. Before finding her spouse, Deuber dated some big-name celebrities, and she apparently had a type: athletes, particularly ultra-famous and champion athletes.

"I dated Lance Armstrong for, like, six months," Deuber told Life & Style in 2017, referring to the multiple Tour de France winning cyclist, cancer survivor, Livestrong founder, and ultimately admitted and disgraced user of performance-enhancing drugs (according to USA Today). Deuber called their romance "very fun and kind of easy," and said that Armstrong called it off before things got too serious, as he was hesitant to jump into something major after the end of his engagement to Sheryl Crow.

Very soon after she and Armstrong broke up, Deuber had a dalliance with New York Yankees star Derek Jeter. "I went on, like, literally two dates with the guy," Deuber said. "In New York, we went out once and we went out once in Dallas and that was it. He wasn't my cup of tea — and nor was I his, I think."
