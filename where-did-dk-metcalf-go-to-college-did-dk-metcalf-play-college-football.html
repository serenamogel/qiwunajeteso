DeKaylin Zecharius “DK” Metcalf is an American football wide receiver for the Seattle Seahawks of the National Football League (NFL).

Where did DK Metcalf go to college? Did DK Metcalf play college football?

DK Metcalf attended the Oxford High School in Oxford, Mississippi, where he had 224 receptions for 3,302 yards and 49 touchdowns.

After his high school career, he committed to play college football at the University of Mississippi. During his freshman year in 2016, he appeared in the first two games of the season.

However, he suffered a season-ending foot injury and was granted a redshirt following his injury. During the 2018 season, he had 26 receptions for 569 yards before sustaining another season-ending injury. This time, it was a neck injury against Arkansas.

After his 2018 season, he declared for the 2019 NFL Draft, effectively forgoing the last two years of his collegiate career. In the second round with the 64th overall pick in the 2019 NFL Draft, he was selected by the Seattle Seahawks.
