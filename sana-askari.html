Sana Askari is a well-known, educated and most-talented actress and a model who was born on 23rd April 1988, hailing from Pakistan, grew up in America. While she did schooling from Beaconhouse School. In 2010, she did graduation from NAPA, Karachi and she did her first theatre play “Adhey Adhoore” which was directed and produced under NAPA’s banner. She is one of the most talented Pakistani actresses and models who have been enthralling her fans and audience with her amazing acting skills. Not only this, but she has done various serials for which she gained huge acclaim. While she is mainly known for her recently ended serial “Ghalati” starring Affan Waheed and Hira Mani.

Career

Sana Askari has acted in various TV projects including “Dareecha” in which she worked alongside Imran Aslam and it was aired on ARY Digital. Also, she has worked in “Azar Ki Ayegi Barat”, “Dolly Ki Ayegi Barat”, “Khushboo Ka Ghar”, in which she worked alongside Faisal Qureshi and the serial was aired on ARY Digital. Also, she made an appearance in “Ladies Park” in which she worked alongside Azfar Rehman and the serial was aired on Geo TV. Earlier, she appeared in “Mein Abdul Qadir Hoon” in which she worked alongside Fahad Mustafa, Saba Hameed, and Alishba Yousuf. While the serial was aired on Hum TV. Additionally, she made an appearance in some morning shows as a guest, and the shows include “Nadia Khan Show”,” Jago Pakistan”, “Good Morning Pakistan” and others are worth sharing. Also, she took part in the reality TV show “Fear Factor Pakistan” but unfortunately, she was eliminated. Among other popular serials “Ary Digital’s “Kaash Aisa Ho”, “ARY Digital’s “Timmy G”, “Daagh”, “Meri Dulari”, “Kitni GIrhen Baaqi Hain”, “Madventures”, “Who Dubara”, and others are included. However, she has been constantly entertaining her fans and viewers with her amazing performances.
