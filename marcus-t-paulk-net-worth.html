Marcus T. Paulk Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Marcus Terrell Paulk (born October 12, 1986) is an American actor, rapper and dancer best known for his role as Myles Mitchell in the UPN sitcom Moesha, which aired from 1996 through 2001. Being a proponent of good causes and a frequent participant in fund raisers, Paulk was the 1997 national spokesperson for the "Kids Are Paramount" campaign, which seeks to empower children with courage and confidence to overcome obstacles.He also appeared with Bow Wow in the 2005 film Roll Bounce, Another Cinderella Story as Dustin, and with Antonio Banderas in the 2006 film Take the Lead. Paulk is currently working on a studio album. Further details for this project are TBA. Paulk has also made appearances in Season 4 of The Bad Girls Club. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
