Here's How Emily Ratajkowski Feels About Being the &quot;Mozart of Breasts&quot;

Between her acting and modeling careers, her in-demand Instagram presence, and her months-old marriage, Emily Ratajkowski has a full plate.

Despite the speed at which EmRata’s career has evolved since her breakout in Robin Thicke and Pharrell's 2013 “Blurred Lines” music video, the image of the brunette nude, save for a bold red lip, dancing alongside the fully-clothed male singers is hard to shake.

We were introduced to Emily’s breasts straight out the gate, and her stream of nearly naked Instagram posts have made it difficult to forget.

In a recent interview with Marie Claire, Ratajkowski opened up about her famous assets, revealing that her boobs are a common request in plastic surgeons’ offices.

“Boobs are funny,” she explains. “They hurt sometimes, and sometimes they’re the thing that makes me feel the most powerful. They’re a key to my sexuality. They’re all those things.”

She’s cool with the attention her breasts get, just please leave her dad out of it.

“I remember there was some article like ‘Emily Ratajkowski Is the Mozart of Breasts.’ What was so bad is someone sent it to my dad, who sent it to me. My dad still sees me as a little kid.”

“I love my boobs,” she continued. “I love other people’s boobs. Boobs are kind of great.”

Hear, hear.
