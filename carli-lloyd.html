Is Carli Lloyd Married? Know Her Husband

In case you don't follow women soccer; the National team captain is happily married. She tied the knot in 2016 with Brian Hollins, who is a pro golfer. After winning the world cup, the whole of the United States also celebrated its "Captain America's" engagement. 

 According to The Knot, the couple is a high school sweetheart. After being in a relationship for so long, nuptial is just a formality for the lovely duo. The pair were together for fifteen years and are head over heels in love. In January 2015, Hollins popped up the big question with a cushion-cut halo and a triple shank diamond band.

They walked down the aisle on November 4, 2016, after the Rio Olympics. For the big day, the pair took a trip to Puerto Morelos, Mexico, in the company of their family. 

Carli Lloyd's Net Worth

Most of her wealth comes from her brand endorsement rather than her salary. Lloyd's wealth is around $500,000 as per online sources. Her income ranges from $10,000 to $30,000.

After winning the world cup, the women's team, including Lloyd, received a sum of $2 million and a further $75,000 bonus. Her fortune can be compared to another team-mate, Adrianna Franch, who receives a payout of $16,000 to $45,000.

In addition to her national team, she played in club football as well. Her brand endorsement adds more fortune to her assets. She had a deal with Nike for several years and also endorses for Pressure Makes Us: Carli Lloyd. Furthermore, she starred in the Xfinity commercial and also signed a contract with Visa. 

Some of her other brands are Lifeway, Heineken, United Airlines, NJM Insurance, and Beats, to name a few. Furthermore, she previously modeled for Sports Illustrated, Glamour, and Shape magazine. From her modeling and brand endorsements, her wealth might've increased ten folds.

Lloyd thinking about NFL

After completing the 2020 Olympics, Lloyd is seriously thinking about starting her kicking career in NFL. In the radio interview, her kicking of the ball was not a publicity stunt and wants to think about her next move.

Another legendary NFL player, Tom Brady, also weighed in on Lloyd, saying she has the potential of kicking field goals in NFL. So far, a couple of NFL teams have approached the World Cup winner, but she is currently focused on the Olympics in 2020.

Her Early Life, Age, and Social Media

Born on July 16, 1982, Lloyd is thirty-seven-years-old as of 2019. The 5 feet 7-inch tall Midfielder grew up alongside her siblings in Delran Township, New Jersey, USA.

Her father, Stephen Lloyd and Mother Pamela Lloyd raised her well and supported her soccer career from the early age of five. She played a lot of soccer and even became a key member of her high school team.      

After being named Courier-Post player of the year in 2000, she started her professional career for Rutgers Scarlet Knights in 2001.
