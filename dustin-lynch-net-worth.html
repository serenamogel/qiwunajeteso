Dustin Lynch | Career

Lynch began his career with Valory Music Group but departed in 2011 after following one of the company’s executives to Broken Bow Records.

He recorded and released “Cowboys and Angels,” his first and most successful single, which reached No. 2 on Country Airplay and was later certified platinum.

The song was also nominated for two American Country Awards, which helped him significantly raise his net worth and establish his career.

In August 2012, Dustin released his self-titled first album, which swiftly soared to the top of the Top Country Albums chart.

His second album, titled “Where It’s At,” sold over 138,000 copies which helped bolster his fortune even more.

‘Seein’ Red’ was the lead single off his third album, ‘Current Mood,’ released in 2016. “Current Mood” debuted at No. 2 on the Billboard Country chart and No. 7 on the Billboard 200.

Dustin Lynch was nominated for the CMT Music Awards in 2013 and the Academy of Country Music Awards a year later in the Top New Artist of the Year category.

In 2013, he was crowned Best New Country Music Artist by Elle magazine.
